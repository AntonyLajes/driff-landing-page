Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Driff (“Driff”, “we”, “us” — operated by the Driff Team) handles personal data collected through this website and the Driff beta waitlist. By using this site or joining the waitlist, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Waitlist details you provide: name, email address, team or company name, team size, role, and an optional GitHub organization link.
  • Usage analytics: anonymous product/usage events (e.g. page views, button clicks, language). We do not send your name or email to our analytics provider, and we do not collect your IP address for analytics.
  • Technical data: standard server logs generated by our hosting provider.

How we use it

  • To manage the closed beta waitlist and contact you when a spot opens.
  • To send you a confirmation and beta-related communications.
  • To understand how the site is used and improve the product.

Service providers

We share data only with providers that help us operate the service:

  • Railway — hosting and database (United States).
  • Resend — transactional and beta emails.
  • Mixpanel — privacy-conscious product analytics (no name/email/IP).

Data is stored and processed in the United States.

Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage only to remember your language preference and for basic analytics. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Email communications

We only email you about the beta and your waitlist status. Every email includes a way to opt out, and we will not sell or rent your information.

Data retention

We keep your waitlist information until the beta concludes or until you ask us to delete it, whichever comes first.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@driff.dev. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

Driff is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We'll revise the “Last updated” date above when we do.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@driff.dev.
